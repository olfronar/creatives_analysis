# Pattern Library Prompt

## Input

- Multiple validated deconstruction reports.
- Multiple adaptation reports.

## Task

Synthesize cross-video patterns without overfitting to a small sample.

## Required Output

- Repeated hook formulas.
- Repeated promise types.
- Proof and demo mechanisms.
- Caption and on-screen text patterns.
- Edit rhythm patterns.
- CTA patterns.
- Platform-specific lessons.
- Mechanism cards: mechanism name, evidence lineage, safe translation, what cannot be copied.
- Submagic adaptation backlog.
- Risks, uncertainties, and experiments to test.

## Constraints

- Do not call patterns "winning" without performance metrics.
- Separate repeated mechanisms from isolated stylistic details.
- Include at least one hypothesis per pattern.
- Preserve angle diversity; do not collapse all ideas into speed, captions, and B-roll.
