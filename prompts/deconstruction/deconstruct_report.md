# Deconstruct Report Prompt

## Input

- Evidence packet.
- Short-form creative rubric.
- Platform fit rubric.

## Task

Produce a complete competitor creative deconstruction for one video.

## Required Output

1. Evidence summary.
2. Timeline breakdown: `0-3s`, `3-6s`, `6-15s`, midpoint, CTA/end.
3. Core story or idea.
4. Rubric scores from `0` to `3`.
5. Strengths.
6. Weaknesses.
7. Likely drop-off points.
8. Platform fit matrix.
9. Submagic adaptation opportunities at the mechanism level only.
10. Risks and unknowns.

## Constraints

- Do not claim the creative performed well without metrics.
- Every score needs evidence and confidence.
- Do not invent unseen product features, results, or platform data.
- Keep Submagic ideas at the opportunity level; full scripts belong in the adaptation prompt.
