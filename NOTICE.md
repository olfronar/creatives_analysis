# Publication Notice

This repository is a creative-analysis workspace for competitor short-form video deconstruction and Submagic adaptation brainstorming.

Source competitor videos are not included. The `creatives/` directory is ignored and is intended for local-only MP4 inputs.

Committed `outputs/` examples may contain sampled still frames, OCR snippets, brand names, and analysis derived from publicly reviewed short-form ads. These materials are included only for creative-analysis reference, evidence lineage, and HTML report-format review.

The reports are analytical artifacts, not endorsements, performance claims, or official brand guidelines. Creative scores are inferred signal judgments unless real platform metrics are supplied.

Do not reuse competitor scripts, visuals, captions, music, creator likenesses, brand identities, proprietary claims, or source videos from this workspace. Adapt mechanisms and strategy only.
