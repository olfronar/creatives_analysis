# Platform Fit Rubric

Use this rubric after the core creative deconstruction. Each platform gets a short note and `0-3` fit score.

## TikTok

- Native creator feel.
- Fast hook in the first 1-3 seconds.
- Strong captions and sound behavior.
- Visual movement that works in a scrolling feed.
- Trend usage only if it supports the product promise.

## Instagram Reels

- Clean vertical framing.
- Safe lower zone for UI controls.
- Captions readable on mobile.
- Strong first frame and clear product/result visibility.
- CTA does not feel disconnected from the story.

## YouTube Shorts

- Promise understandable without external context.
- Good retention through the middle, not only a strong opener.
- Caption and audio clarity.
- End CTA fits Shorts viewing behavior.

## Meta Paid Social

- First-frame thumb-stop.
- Product/problem visible early.
- Proof or demo appears before attention fades.
- CTA can support conversion, not only engagement.

## LinkedIn

- Business pain is explicit enough for professional context.
- Promise connects to output, workflow, team, or agency economics.
- Visuals remain mobile-readable.
- CTA fits a B2B or creator-business decision path.
